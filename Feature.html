Local Sourcing

We partner with local fishermen in Subic and Masinloc to get the freshest crabs daily. Our crabs are caught using sustainable methods and transported live to our kitchen.

Quality Standards

Every crab must pass our 5-point inspection: lively movement, intact shell, proper weight, clean smell, and bright color. Only about 60% of deliveries meet our strict standards.

Perfect Timing

Our chefs have perfected the exact cooking time for each crab size - 8 minutes for small, 10 for medium, and 12 for large. This ensures tender, juicy meat every time.

Community Connection

Crab King is more than a restaurant — we support local fishermen, provide jobs, and give back to the community through events and partnerships.

Chef’s Expertise

Our seasoned chefs combine traditional Filipino cooking with modern techniques, bringing out the best flavors of every seafood dish.
